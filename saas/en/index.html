FAQ

Questions?

Can I switch plans at any time? +
Yes. Upgrade to Pro or downgrade to Free whenever you like. Your data is always kept safe.
Is my data secure? +
Data is stored encrypted on Supabase servers (EU). We never share your data with third parties.
Does it work without internet? +
Yes. The app stores data locally on your device and syncs to the cloud when you reconnect.
Is there a mobile app? +
FuneralOS is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — you can install it from your browser on iOS or Android without going through an App Store.
What happens if I hit 5 ceremonies on the free plan? +
You'll be prompted to upgrade to Pro. Your existing data is never deleted or locked.
Do you support Orthodox / Greek funeral workflows? +
Yes. The app was originally built for Greek Orthodox funeral offices and supports burial, cremation, 3-year plot management and more.
